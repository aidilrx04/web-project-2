Pulau Langkawi

Price: RM 13 - 60

Langkawi is one of the districts in the state of Kedah Darul Aman, Malaysia. The island was given duty-free status in 1987. Today, this island rich in natural beauty and legends of the past has presented itself as one of the most popular tourist destinations in Malaysia. Langkawi is a group of islands where Langkawi Island is the largest followed by Banggi Island and Penang Island.

Black Sand Beach (Pantai Pasir Hitam) offers memorable photo opportunities and a peaceful retreat from Langkawi's busy beaches. It's just 4 kilometres southwest of Tanjung Rhu Beach, around a 20-minute drive from the Langkawi Cable Car. Black Sand Beach is a popular hangout spot among locals, especially during weekends and public holidays. Despite its name, the beach is actually a mix of black and white sand.
